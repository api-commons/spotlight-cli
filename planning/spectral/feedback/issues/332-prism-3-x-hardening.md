# Prism 3.x Hardening 

The purpose of this Epic is to track the progress of work related to testing Prism v3 binary (e2e).Finishing this epic would mean that we have the coverage for at least the scenarios that Prism v2 supports.Those e2e tests will run in CI and will guarantee stability and quality of Prism.
