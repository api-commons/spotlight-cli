# Announcing: Introduction to Spectral Webinar

At Stoplight, we promote consistent API design and best practices.  APIs should have style guides with rules and be reviewed to ensure they are descriptive, readable, and concise for the developers who use them.

That’s why we’re excited to announce our much improved, flexible JSON linter, Spectral! 

With Spectral you can:

- Create custom rules to lint JSON or YAML objects
- Have out of the box rules to validate and lint OpenAPI v2 and v3 documents
- Use JSON path to apply rules to specific parts of your objects
- And there’s much more!

It will be on July 15, 2019 10:00 AM in Central Time with @philsturgeon and Justin Forte.

Sign up at https://zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_9rokGnkiRN6t8kSOmuUpwQ

See you there!
